Thailand Bangkok Temple

The Thailand Bangkok Temple is Under Construction. First announced on April 5, 2015 by President Monson. The site was dedicated and ground broken on January 26, 2019. The temple has since been under construction. It is estimated to be completed in early to mid-2023.
